Aries

Basic Information

Dates: March 21 - April 19

Element: Fire

Quality: Cardinal

Ruling Planet: Mars

Symbol: The Ram

Personality Traits

  • Courageous and energetic
  • Natural-born leader
  • Confident and determined
  • Enthusiastic and optimistic
  • Dynamic and quick-witted

Description

Aries is the first sign of the zodiac, and that's exactly how they like to be - first! Their bold and ambitious nature makes them natural leaders. They're known for their energetic and adventurous spirit, always ready to take on new challenges with enthusiasm and determination.

As a Fire sign ruled by Mars, Aries individuals are dynamic, spontaneous, and possess a warrior spirit. They have an infectious enthusiasm that makes them natural motivators of others. While they may be quick to anger, they're equally quick to forgive and don't tend to hold grudges.
